# Funding & Business Model

Zveltio is MIT-licensed open source. **Self-hosted only** — we don't and won't offer cloud hosting. Your data stays on your infrastructure, on your terms.

## How the project sustains itself

- **Community donations** — GitHub Sponsors, OpenCollective.
- **A marketplace of paid business extensions** — verticals and integrations published by the core team and third-party authors. Buying an extension funds its author and the platform.
- **Professional support and SLA contracts** — coming once the project sustains a team. For organizations that need guaranteed response times, on-call engineers, or installation/maintenance support.

## What we promise

- The core platform stays open source under MIT — forever.
- The data formats stay open and exportable — always.
- No tracking, no telemetry that leaves the user's installation without consent.
- No cloud-hosted version that competes with self-hosted users.

## What we don't promise

- That every feature stays free forever. Some advanced or specialized capabilities may live in paid extensions or, in the future, a paid "Enterprise" edition with extras.
- That you'll get free support. Community help via GitHub issues is best-effort.
