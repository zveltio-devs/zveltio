# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 13, 2026
**Last updated:** March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Zveltio ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our software, website, and Developer Portal.

---

## 1. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to:

- The **Zveltio website** (zveltio.com)
- The **Zveltio Developer Portal** (developer.zveltio.com)
- The **Zveltio Marketplace**

> **Self-hosted deployments**: When you run Zveltio on your own infrastructure, you are the data controller. We have no access to data processed by your self-hosted instance. This policy does not apply to data your own users submit to your Zveltio instance.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Developer Portal Account

When you create an account on the Developer Portal, we collect:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Name | Account identification |
| Email address | Authentication, account communications |
| Password (hashed) | Authentication — never stored in plain text |
| OAuth provider ID | If you sign in via GitHub or Google |
| Company name | Optional, for invoicing and license management |

### 2.2 License & Billing

When you purchase commercial extensions or licenses:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Payment information | Processed by Stripe — we do not store card details |
| Billing address | Invoice generation, tax compliance |
| Transaction records | License verification, accounting |
| License keys | Extension activation and validation |

We use **Stripe** as our payment processor. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data. We never store full card numbers or CVVs.

### 2.3 Usage Data

We may collect limited, anonymized usage data from the Developer Portal:

- Pages visited, features used
- Error logs (without personal data)
- API request metadata (endpoint, response codes — no request/response bodies)

We do **not** use third-party analytics trackers (e.g., Google Analytics) on the Developer Portal.

### 2.4 Communications

If you contact us (email, support), we retain the content of those communications to respond and improve our service.

---

## 3. What We Do Not Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection:

- **No telemetry** from self-hosted Zveltio instances
- **No tracking pixels** or behavioral advertising
- **No selling of data** to third parties — ever
- **No profiling** for advertising purposes

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively to:

1. **Provide the service** — manage your account, activate licenses, process payments
2. **Communicate with you** — send transactional emails (account confirmation, receipts, security alerts)
3. **Improve the product** — analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns
4. **Legal compliance** — meet tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations
5. **Security** — detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## 6. Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

We use trusted third-party processors who operate under strict data processing agreements:

- **Stripe** — payment processing

### 6.3 Business Transfers

## 7. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | Duration of account + 90 days after deletion request |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal / tax requirement) |
| License records | Duration of license validity + 2 years |
| Support communications | 3 years |
| Access logs | 90 days |

You may request deletion of your account at any time (see Section 9). Payment records may be retained longer due to legal obligations.

---

## 8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in transit**: All connections use TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at rest**: Sensitive data is encrypted in our database
- **Password hashing**: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
- **Access control**: Strict internal access controls; employees access data on a need-to-know basis
- **Dependency auditing**: Regular security audits of our codebase and dependencies

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. We will notify you promptly in the event of a data breach that affects your personal data.

## 9. Your Rights (GDPR & Similar)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

### 9.1 Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 9.2 Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 9.3 Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of your personal data. Note: some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes (e.g., payment records).

### 9.4 Data Portability
Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).

### 9.5 Restriction of Processing
Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

### 9.6 Objection
Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### 9.7 Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent (e.g., marketing emails) at any time.

**To exercise any of these rights**, contact us at **privacy@zveltio.com**. We will respond within 30 days. For EEA residents, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
